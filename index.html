Overview

Nature of Remote Work

  • Remote work lacks the natural communication that happens in offices.
  • Leaders must be more intentional about building connections and setting clear expectations.
  • Teams should use a mix of communication tools—like Zoom for live discussions and Slack or Teams for asynchronous communication.

Relational Impacts

  • Remote work significantly alters how communication occurs in organizations.
  • Heavy reliance on technology
  • Slower response times

Financial Benefits

  • Companies save on office space, utilities, and overhead costs.
  • Increases profit margins by reducing physical infrastructure needs.
  • Remote work boosts employee satisfaction and loyalty.
